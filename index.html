About TakeOn

TakeOn was established in 2018 by five young developers with a goal to disrupt the recruiting industry. Today we're privileged enough to be changing lives everyday. We match graduates and potential employers but we do more than just recruitment; We build relationships. We ensure that every match between a graduate and an employer is a match made in heaven.

What's our vision?

We're looking to revolutionize the recruiting process by making recruiting more personal than ever.

Why choose us?

We are a team of developers with a strong vision of a better digital world. Using powerful algorithms, we will provide you with the best career choices.

How will I benefit?

Job offers and career guidance for you.
